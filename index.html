Project Life v2 Revision 2 Released

Download it here.

Hello everyone! We finished making revision 2 of Project Life v2. The major things in this revision is that it fixes the majority of known bugs, such as glass and smoke textures screwing up, as well as fixing the chaper text so it actually shows the chapter instead of the #SMOD_..., and some sounds were fixed and added in to the mod.

Revision 3 will have a reduced filesize, as well as add custom sounds for the weapons, as well as a few rebalances, such as more predictable recoil patterns, akin to games such as Insurgency and Rainbow Six: Vegas.

Changelog:

Removed buggy CS:S glass textures
Fixed smoke texture error
Fixed #SMOD_Chapter... bug
Fixed missing sounds
Added additional sounds

Current Known Issues:

- cs_office, cs_assault, and the custom hostage maps have hostages dying before the game starts, player dying, spawns are incorrect.
